# Retail-Sales-analysis
An end-to-end Power BI retail analytics project transforming raw sales data into actionable insights for FMCG market share, SKU performance, and channel distribution.

Project Overview: This project involves a comprehensive analysis of retail sales data to track market size, volume trends, and brand performance within the FMCG sector. By leveraging Power BI, I transformed complex datasets into a multi-page interactive dashboard that allows stakeholders to monitor monthly growth and competitive positioning.

Key Features & Insights:
Market Size Analysis: Visualized monthly sales value (PKR) and volume (Tons) trends to identify seasonal peaks and market fluctuations.
Channel Distribution: Analyzed volume shares across various retail channels, including Bakery, LMT, and General Trade, to determine the most effective distribution paths.
SKU Performance: Identified top-performing SKUs (such as Bombay Biryani and Qorma Masala sachets) and analyzed their contribution to total sales volume.
Brand Comparison: Evaluated "Top Brand" performance against competitors to highlight market leadership and growth opportunities.

Technical Stack:
Data Visualization: Power BI Desktop.
Data Modeling: DAX for custom measures and KPI calculations.
Data Processing: Power Query for cleaning and validating retail data flows.
